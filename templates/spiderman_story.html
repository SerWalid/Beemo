Spiderman - The Friendly Hero!

Once upon a time, there was a boy named Peter Parker. Peter wasn’t just any boy—he was special! One day, a tiny spider bit Peter, and guess what happened? Peter turned into Spider-Man! Now, he can climb walls and swing high in the sky with his sticky spider webs.

Spider-Man loves to help people in the city. When someone is in trouble, Spider-Man swoops in to save the day! He uses his web to catch the bad guys and keep everyone safe. People in the city feel happy because they know Spider-Man is there to protect them.

One day, a big, mean villain named Green Goblin tried to scare everyone. But Spider-Man wasn’t afraid. He used his webs to stop Green Goblin and made the city safe again.

Even though Peter is just a regular kid, when he puts on his Spider-Man suit, he becomes a true hero. Spider-Man always remembers to be kind and help others. And that’s what makes him a great hero. The end!
