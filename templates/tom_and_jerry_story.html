Tom & Jerry - The Silly Chase!

Meet Tom, a big, fluffy cat, and Jerry, a tiny, sneaky mouse. Tom is always trying to catch Jerry, but Jerry is super clever and always finds a way to escape! It’s like a big game of hide-and-seek in the house.

One day, Tom built a trap to catch Jerry, but Jerry zoomed through the house, making silly faces at Tom. Tom tried to chase him, but whoops! He tripped and slid right into a pile of pillows! Jerry giggled and squeaked, “Better luck next time, Tom!”

Even though they chase each other all the time, deep down, Tom and Jerry are the best of friends. They always end up playing and having fun together, no matter how many times Tom tries to catch Jerry. The end!
